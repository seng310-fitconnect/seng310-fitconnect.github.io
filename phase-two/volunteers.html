Requirement: Volunteers need to be able to sign up and be contacted so that they can complete training and participate.

When a user signs up for FitConnect, they can choose to sign up as a volunteer or a participant. If the user chooses to sign up as a volunteer, he/she will be contacted by the UVHS staff. The UVHS staff will schedule a meeting with further instructions to ensure that the volunteer is there for the correct reasons. From there, the volunteer will need to complete the training the UVHS staff has recommended. After the completed training, the volunteer will be able to login to the FitConnect app using his/hers email and password they set up when signing up for the program. The volunteer will now be able to make matches and be able to chat with participants he/she has matched with.

Requirement: Volunteers need to know how to to appropriately help participants.

Volunteers have the ability to see their matches and their fitness goals, so they will be able to better help participants. They are also provided with chat functionality, so they can further inquire. Upon sign up, volunteers will need to complete training which will help them learn how to appropriately help. After the training is complete, the volunteer will receive an email, confirming that their account is now active. The volunteer will now be able to login, and start matching with participants.
