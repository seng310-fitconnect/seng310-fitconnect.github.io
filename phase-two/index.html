Introduction

FitConnect is a mobile application that matches participants with a potential fitness volunteer based on the participant's requirements and availability. The app promotes fitness through a safe and friendly environment where the application process of volunteers and participants is monitored by the administrative staff of the University of Victoria Health Services (UVHS).

This website contains low-fidelity mockups and design models for the proposed project. Based on the developments from phase I, in phase II the specific details entailing the applications were discussed and implemented. For instance, design prototypes for administration, volunteers and participants all display features of the application that allows ease of access, consistency and minimalistic design to all its users.
